Direct Delivery with Flat Rates for a 26-Foot Truck

Our direct delivery service offers a dedicated 26-foot truck exclusively for your move. Unlike consolidated moves where shipments are combined, your belongings are the only items on the truck, ensuring no shared space and no delays from other deliveries.
With flat-rate pricing, you'll have a clear, all-inclusive cost for your move, covering everything from loading to delivery.

Benefits of Direct Delivery:
  • Exclusive use of the truck - no sharing space with other customers
  • Faster delivery - no additional stops or detours
  • Flat-rate pricing - no hidden fees or surprises
  • Dedicated team - from loading to delivery, your belongings get personalized care
  • Streamlined process - more control over timing and delivery
